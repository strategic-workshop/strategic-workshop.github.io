<h2 class="page-title">Programme</h2>

<!-- Intro -->
<p class="programme-intro">
In an increasingly connected world, Global Digitization is reshaping the mode of operation, communication
and innovation of societies. It has the potential to bridge the gap across geographies and promote inclusion in every sector from education and healthcare to energy, industry, and governance. However, embracing this transformation necessitates addressing digital equity, data sovereignty, and ethical use of technology. To address these dimensions, joint efforts are required from governments, industry, and academia to establish strong digital ecosystems, secure technological frameworks, and sustainable energy networks. The future of global digitization depends on joint collaboration and shared vision to ensure sustainable, secure, and accessible digital growth for all.
</p>
